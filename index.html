About Me

Hello, i'm Sydney.

I am a designer and web developer living in Calgary, AB. Three years ago, mid Covid I moved to Calgary to study at Alberta University of the Arts. Between my third and fourth year I caught a show in The Illingworth Kerr Gallery on a whim. It was a collection of work themed around AI, machine learning, surveillance, and ethical tech problems. Here I was introduced to how art and technology could come together. I was hooked, and it completely changed my process and my focus. I was featured in three public art shows in Calgary between 2022 and 2023 where I was prominently mixing art and computer tech in my work. The success of these projects led to my winning the Board of Governors Graduating Student Award in 2023. Moving forward I am still combining art and tech in creating responsive, well-designed web pages. I love learning new things and experimenting. When I'm not in school, I'm working on personal projects and illustrations, or perfecting my crochet techniques. If you have a web problem that requires a creative solution, reach out! I am actually very cool.
